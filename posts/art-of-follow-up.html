The Art of the Follow-Up: Converting Inquiries

The money is in the follow-up. It's a cliché because it's true. Most institutes call a lead once, get no answer, and mark it as "Dead." This is throwing money away.

The "Speed to Lead" Rule

If you call a parent within 5 minutes of them submitting an inquiry, your chances of conversion go up by 900%. Yes, 900%. If you wait 24 hours, you are forgotten. Automate this. Use tools to instantly alert your counselor when a lead comes in.

Use Multiple Channels

They didn't pick up the phone? Send a WhatsApp. "Hi Mrs. Sharma, saw you inquired about the JEE batch. Is now a good time to talk?" It's non-intrusive and gets a high reply rate. Use SMS for reminders. Use Email for newsletters.

Value, Not Spam

Don't just call to ask "Are you joining?" Call to offer value. "Hi, we are hosting a free career counseling seminar this Saturday. Would you like to attend?" This moves them down the funnel without being pushy.

The 2-2-2 Rule

Call 2 days after the first inquiry. Then 2 days after that. Then 2 weeks later. Persistence beats luck every time.
