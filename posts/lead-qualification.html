Stop Wasting Money: Qualify Your Leads First

"I want 1000 leads." We hear this every day. And every day we ask: "Can you handle 1000 phone calls?" Most institutes can't.

Volume is Vanity. Quality is Sanity.

100 leads who can't afford you are worthless compared to 10 leads who are ready to pay. Stop optimizing for "Cheap Cost Per Lead." Optimize for "Cost Per Enrolled Student."

The Qualification Framework

Add friction to your forms. Don't just ask for Name/Number. Ask: "What is your budget for coaching?" or "What is your student's current grade?" This filters out the tire kickers instantly.

Train Your Counselors

Your counselors should not be order takers. They should be doctors. They need to diagnose the student's problem before prescribing your course. If they just say "Fees are 50k," you lose. If they say "Let's see if this course fits your goals," you win respect.

Key Takeaway

It's better to have a small list of high-intent parents than a massive database of dead numbers. Clean your lists.
