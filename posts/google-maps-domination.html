How to Dominate Your Local Area on Google Maps

If someone searches "Coaching center near me," do you show up first? If not, you are handing students to your competitors. Local SEO (Google Maps) is the highest-converting channel for institutes because it captures intent at the exact moment of need.

Reviews are Oxygen

Quantity matters, but quality matters more. A 5-star review with no text is weak. A 5-star review that says "Sharma Sir is the best Physics teacher because..." is gold. Incentivize your current students to mention specific subjects and teachers in their reviews.

Photos Drive Clicks

Google confirms that profiles with photos receive 42% more requests for directions. Upload photos of your exterior, your classrooms, your library, and your events. Keep it fresh. Upload 2-3 new photos every week.

NAP Consistency

Name, Address, Phone Number (NAP). Ensure these are exactly the same on your website, your Facebook, your JustDial, and your Google Profile. Inconsistencies confuse Google's algorithm and lower your ranking.

The Golden Rule

Treat your Google Business Profile like a social media feed. Post updates, offers, and news there regularly. Active profiles rank higher.
