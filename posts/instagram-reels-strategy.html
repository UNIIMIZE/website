Instagram Reels for Coaching Centers: What Works?

Every institute knows they need to be on Instagram. But most are just posting festival greetings (#HappyDiwali) and topper photos. That's not a strategy. That's a notice board. To grow, you need Reels.

Idea 1: The "Trick" Video

Have your math teacher solve a complex problem in 30 seconds using a shortcut. These videos go viral because they provide instant value and show off faculty competence.

Idea 2: Student Vox-Pops

Ask students simple, fun questions during break. "What's the hardest subject?" "Who's your favorite teacher?" It shows the culture of your institute, which attracts other students.

Idea 3: Myth Busting

"Myth: You need to study 16 hours to crack NEET. Fact: It's about efficiency, not hours." Controversial takes stop the scroll.

"Your content is an audition for your classroom."

Consistency is Key

You can't post once a month. The algorithm rewards consistency. Post one Reel every two days. It doesn't have to be high production. Raw, authentic content often performs better.
